1. Create a client certificate.
2. Export it (*.pfx) and add it to the project's "Certificates" folder.
3. Update the CertificateFileName variable in MainPage.xaml.cs with the certificate file name.

More info:
https://support.quovadisglobal.com/KB/a46/exporting-a-pfx-using-mmc.aspx
